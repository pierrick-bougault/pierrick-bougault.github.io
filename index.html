01 / About

Research focus — where AI meets the factory floor.

I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Industrial Engineering, Tsinghua University (Beijing), working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, lean quality management, and human–computer interaction in industrial settings.

My research explores how agentic AI systems and large language models can augment — rather than replace — the tacit expertise of engineers and operators on aircraft assembly lines.
